About 2020 And My Resolutions For 2021



About 2020



2020 had been a drastic year, hadn't it? Its was a year we all desperately
wanted to get over with. I can't even explain how it affected me. I can't actually
believe that I miss school!! I also have started getting really really cranky. I can't
go to play, I can't have friends over, what can I do? But thankfully, that dreadful
year is now behind us. And I hope that 2021 is WAYYYYYYYYY better than its younger
brother.







My Resolutions For 2021


1. As Covid-19 is still not over, I resolve to keep myself healthy, hygenic and safe.

2. I want to learn how to cook, because who knows? I could be sent to boarding school in some months.

3. I resolve to exercise daily. It helps us be healthy, strong and happy :)

4. I want to code a new game every 3-4 months.

5. I resolve to control my anger and crankiness so that it doesn't affect others and me.
